PLACES TO VISIT NEAR BPGC

  1. BOGMALLO (7 kms): Half of the campus can be found at this beach on saturdays and sundays.

        If you are a fitness enthusiast then you can jog till the beach and sit and enjoy the sunset while sipping water (because you are a fitness enthusiast :p)

A bus ride till bogmallo would cost you about 20 Rupees.

There are a lot of cool restaurants around this place, which includes:

John Seagull

Claudi’s corner

Joet’s

And many more

2. Sao Jacinto Island (6 kms)

Again, joggable.

Restaurants to try: Sheela Bar & Restaurant

3. Heart Shaped Lake(Approx 8 kms)

4. Dudhsagar falls, if you are a trekking enthusiast (70 kms)

College’s trekking club usually organises expeditions to Dudhsagar falls every year, so you might as well join them.

                

5.Three Kings Chapel Chapel (Approx 6 kms)

It’s considered to be a haunted place. So go explore after the sun sets.

6. Japanese Garden (13 kms)
